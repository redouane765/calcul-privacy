Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 2026

1 Overview

Calcul is a simple and professional calculator application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

✓ No data collection

2 Data We Do NOT Collect

Calcul does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This includes:

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or statistics
  • No calculation history sent to any server

3 Local Storage Only

All data (calculation history, memory values, notes) is stored locally on your device only using the app's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never shared with anyone.

4 No Internet Connection Required

Calcul works completely offline. The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers.

5 No Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics tools, or tracking services.

6 Children's Privacy

Calcul is safe for all ages. Since we collect no personal data, there are no privacy concerns for users of any age, including children under 13.
